Cup Of Tea

Description

Do you need a hot cup of flavoured water with an added splash of milk?

Then look no further as we tell how to make one.

Ingredients

  • cup
  • Tea bag of your choice
  • milk
  • hot water
  • sugar (optional)
  • Biscuits ( becauase why not?)

Steps

  1. Get cup and place tea bag inside
  2. Boil water in kettle
  3. once boiled pour in cup over teabag and fill 3/4 full
  4. let bag steep for 3 mins
  5. remove bag and add milk
  6. dunk biscuits
