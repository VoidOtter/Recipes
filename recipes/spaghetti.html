Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A lovely slice of Italian life in a bowl

There are many variations of ingredients but we will be sitcking to the classic Beef

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 1kg mince
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cholula sauce
  • parmesan
  • bolognese sauce

Steps

  1. Brown off the mince, add a splash of cholula sauce and a little salt and pepper
  2. Put spaghetti in pan and boil until soft
  3. Add sauce to mince and boil
  4. Once cooked, drain spaghetti and place in bowl
  5. Place mince and sauce over spaghetti
  6. cover with parmesan
