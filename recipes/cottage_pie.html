Cottage Pie

Description

A simple but hearty classic that warms the cockles and is very simple to make.

There are many variations of ingredients but we will be sitcking to the classic carrots & peas

Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 1kg mince
  • 500g carrots
  • 150g peas
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Beef gravy with 500 - 700ml of water
  • Butter and milk(splash of)

Steps

  1. Brown off the mince, add a splash of worcestershire sauce and a little salt and pepper
  2. Peel and chop the potatoes and boil until soft
  3. Peel and chop carrots and boil, adding peas
  4. make gravy and add to cooked mince
  5. drain potatoes, add butter and splash of milk and then mash until soft
  6. preheat oven 200c
  7. put cooked carrots and peas into mince and bring to the boil
  8. empty cooked mince into deep over proof dish
  9. put potatoes on top and flatten and score with fork
  10. cook for 20 mins until top crispy
